About IITK and campus life

Established in 1959,IIT Kanpur has consistently been a pioneer in the field of engineering education and research.With a wide campus, the institute has evolved into a hub where bright minds come as one to explore,create and shape the future. The institute had modern research facilities and it also hosts vibrant cultural festivals.
